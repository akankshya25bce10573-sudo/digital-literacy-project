***++Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students++***  
***++Do’s (Responsible Practices)++***  
* Maintain a Professional Image   Share content that reflects your values, skills, and personality positively.    
* Think Before You Post   Always consider how your post might be perceived by others, including future employers.    
* Respect Privacy   Seek permission before posting photos, videos, or information about others.    
* Use Strong Security Measures   Enable two-factor authentication and use strong, unique passwords.    
* Verify Information Before Sharing   Ensure that news or information is accurate to avoid spreading misinformation.    
* Engage Positively   Participate in meaningful discussions and support constructive conversations.    
  
  
** *++Don’ts (Practices to Avoid)++***  
  
*  Avoid Sharing Personal Information   Do not disclose sensitive details like address, phone number, or financial data.    
* Do Not Post Offensive or Harmful Content   Avoid content that promotes hate, discrimination, or cyberbullying.    
* Don’t Overshare   Posting excessively can affect your digital reputation and privacy.    
*  Avoid Engaging in Online Arguments   Heated discussions can damage your image and escalate unnecessarily.    
*  Do Not Click Suspicious Links   Avoid unknown links that may lead to phishing or malware attacks.    
*  Don’t Ignore Digital Footprint   Remember that everything posted online can be permanent and traceable.
